About Us

Evangelical Presbyterian Church (EPC) Tongor-Tsanakpe is a vibrant and welcoming community of believers committed to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Located in the heart of Tongor, our church serves as a beacon of hope and service to the local community and beyond. We are passionate about worship, fellowship, and spreading the message of God’s grace.

Whether you are new to the area, searching for a church home, or just visiting, we invite you to join us.

Experience the warmth of our fellowship, the depth of our teaching, and the joy of worshiping together.

Come and be a part of something bigger, as we strive to live out God’s calling in every aspect of our lives.
