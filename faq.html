Welcome to Evangelical Presbyterian Church Your Questions Answered

General Information

Evangelical Presbyterian Church is a Christ-centered church committed to preaching the gospel, living by God’s word, and serving our community in love and faith.

Our church is built on the foundation of the Bible, and we aim to foster a spiritual community Bringing Light to were there is darkness.

Our Sunday service is at 9:00 AM. We also host Evening Thanksgiving service every Fridays at 7:00 PM.

We are located at Tongor, Volta Region, Ghana. Feel free to drop by for a visit, we’d love to welcome you!

About Our Ministries

We have various ministries for all ages, including children’s ministry, and adult Bible studies. Feel free to email us for more information on how you can get involved.
