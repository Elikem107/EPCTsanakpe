Mission

We exist to propagate the Gospel holistically by empowering our members Spiritually, Materially and Socio-Culturally, to be effective agents of the Great Commission

Vision

Our Vision is to be a dynamic agent of God, leading in the Great Commission to bring light to where is darkness and transforming our world into the likeness of Christ and be self-sustaining.

Learn more about us

Our Core Values

1. The Scripture is Central to our faith
2. We want to bear the fruit of the Holy Spirit
3. We believe in Servant-Leadership
4. We uphold Stewardship and Discipleship
5. We are a diversified culture holding democracy
